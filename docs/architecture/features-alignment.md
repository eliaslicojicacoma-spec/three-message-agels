## Feature responsibilities

### home
Landing experience, featured sections, hero, homepage composition.

### bible
Bible navigation, chapter reading, book navigation, progress, search entry points.

### studies
Studies listing, study detail flows, filtering and related studies.

### books
Books listing, book detail flows, metadata, downloads and related books.

### library
User reading history, saved chapters, saved verses, personal reading area.

### downloads
Download listings, file grouping, download cards, filters and categories.

### blog
Blog listing, article page, categories, tags and related posts.

### devotionals
Verse of the day, devotionals listing and devotional detail flows.

### testimonies
Testimony listing, featured testimonies and detail views.

### search
Search UI, filters, search state and result rendering.

### favorites
Favorite verses, favorite chapters, storage integration and saved content views.

### donations
Support page, donation boxes, provider integrations and donation CTAs.

### newsletter
Newsletter forms, validation and newsletter entry components.

### three-angels-message
Mission-specific pages, thematic sections and prophetic content presentation.
